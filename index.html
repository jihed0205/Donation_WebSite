About Us

Tunisia's Most Trusted Donation Platform

Hand2Hand is more than a platform — it's a movement of compassion. We connect people who want to give with associations and individuals who need support.

Every item donated, every job offer posted, every connection made through our platform creates a ripple of real change across Tunisia's communities.

  • 100% free for donors and associations
  • Verified associations across all 24 governorates
  • Transparent donation tracking from giver to recipient
  • Job opportunities posted by partner associations
